Roast Chicken

In this recipe we'll be learning how to cook a delicious roast chicken from scratch. Buckle up - while this recipe is simple you'll need to pay careful attention to the details!

Ingredients

  • One whole, free range chicken
  • 1 Cup of lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Few sprigs fresh thyme
  • 1/2 fresh lemon, cut into wedges
  • 1/4 cup honey, heated to make it easier to brush

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken in salt
  2. Place lemon juice and whole chicken in plastic bag, chill for 2 hours
  3. Preheat the oven to 205 celsius
  4. Pat checken dry, rub with oil add seasonings and lemon wedges
  5. Roast chicken and baste with honey
  6. Tent with foil and let rest
  7. Make gravy
